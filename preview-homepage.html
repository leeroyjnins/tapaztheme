From the Mayor

Welcome to the official portal of the Municipality of Tapaz. Our commitment is to serve our constituents with transparency, efficiency, and dedication. Through this platform, we aim to bring government services closer to you, making it easier for everyone to access the resources and information they need.

Together, we are building a stronger, more prosperous Tapaz. We encourage you to explore our services, stay informed about local events and initiatives, and engage with your local government. Your participation is vital to our community's growth and success.

— Mayor Dante Palomar
